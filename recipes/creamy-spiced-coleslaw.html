Creamy Spiced Coleslaw

  • 1 Cup Mayonnaise
  • 2 Tablespoons Sugar
  • 2 Tablespoons Cider Vinegar
  • 1/2 teaspoon Salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon Pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon Celery Seed
  • 1/2 teaspoon Garlic Powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon Onion Powder
  • 1 (16 ounce) Package Shredded Coleslaw Mix

Steps

  1. Mix mayonnaise, sugar, cider vinegar, salt, pepper, celery seed, garlic powder, and onion powder together in a large bowl. Add coleslaw mix and toss to coat. Cover and chill for at least 1 hour before serving.
